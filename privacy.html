Privacy & Terms

Last updated: 2026-07-02

What StayFinder AI is

StayFinder AI is a travel concierge chat interface. It has a conversation with you, then points you to real, current listings by searching the web. It does not process bookings or payments — every listing links out to the real site (Airbnb, Booking.com, etc.) so you can confirm price and availability yourself before booking anything.

What we send, and to whom

The text you type is sent to our server, which forwards it to Anthropic (the maker of Claude) to generate a reply and, when useful, to run a web search for listings. We don't use this text for anything else.

What we store

We don't run accounts and we don't store your conversations on a server. Saved searches and shared-search links live only in your own browser's local storage — clearing your browser data removes them.

Third parties

Map tiles are provided by Mapbox. Listing links point to third-party sites (Airbnb, Booking.com, etc.) that have their own privacy policies.

Contact
